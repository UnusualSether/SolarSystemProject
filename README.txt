Solar System Project for PUC- Bachelerado em Jogos Digitais 4th Semester, By Lucas Scott

Basic Description-
A simple replica of our solar system containing 8 planets, all with their own editable rotation and obriting speed

How to use-
You can change each individual planet's orbiting and spinning speed by modifying their values through each planet's details tab. (Found on the planet's actor, NOT on the static mesh object)
